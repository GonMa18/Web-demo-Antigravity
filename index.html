Mission

Developing suborbital space launchers

BiSKY Team is the rocketry team of the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). Our goal is to develop suborbital launchers capable of reaching 100 km altitude, transporting micro-experiments and cubesats.

We use hybrid propulsion technology — liquid oxidizer combined with solid fuel — a safer solution that allows for restart and recovery. We follow two research lines: solid combustion rockets for electronics testing, and test benches for hybrid technology.
